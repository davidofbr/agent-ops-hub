## Link Quality Assessment

Ahrefs.com has an exceptional link profile that reflects its position as a leading industry authority in SEO and digital marketing tools. With 1.24 million total backlinks from 78,000 referring domains, the site demonstrates massive scale and authority. The spam score of 4/100 is exceptionally clean—virtually pristine—indicating that virtually all links come from legitimate, high-quality sources. The Domain Rank of 82 out of 100 places ahrefs.com in the elite tier of websites globally.

The profile shows a healthy 12.6% nofollow rate (9,800 out of 78,000 referring domains), which is excellent. The vast majority of links are dofollow, meaning the site receives substantial direct SEO equity from its backlinks. This is the hallmark of a naturally accumulated, earned-link profile rather than artificial link-building campaigns.
